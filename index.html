About Us

Owl Creek Lumber was established in 2001 and is owned and operated by Jeff Turpin. Jeff is a life-long resident of Placer County, has over 15 years experience in hazardous tree removal, is a licenced contractor in the state of California and is a contracted tree faller and hired equipment operator with Cal Fire and the US Forest Service.

Owl Creek is committed to providing outstanding customer service with the highest level of professionalism and expertise:

  • Specializes in Residential Tree Removal and unique chainsaw carvings
  • No job is to small
  • Experienced in large tree crane removal
  • Defensible space fire prevention - brush thinning, chipping and removal

Hazardous Tree Removal

Trees can be hazardous for many resons:

  • Dead
  • Diseased
  • Excessive weight on branches causing unexpected breaking
  • Excessive size
  • Weather damaged (lighting strikes, wind)
  • Proximity to valuable property
  • Multiple tree tops prone to splitting

Proactive removal of trees such as these can save you thousands of dollars in property damage and personal injury and often removal can be more cost less than your insurance deductable. Call Owl Creek for an estimate today.
